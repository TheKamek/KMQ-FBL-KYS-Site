Our Story

Founded in 2020, Keep Your Storage (KYS) emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves access to secure, private digital storage. Our founders, veterans of the cybersecurity industry, recognized the growing need for a storage solution that prioritizes both security and usability.

Today, we serve millions of users worldwide, from individual creators to large enterprises, all united by the need for secure, reliable data storage.

Our Mission

At KYS, our mission is to democratize secure data storage. We believe that robust security shouldn't be a luxury - it should be accessible to everyone. Through continuous innovation and unwavering commitment to privacy, we're building a safer digital world.

We're not just storing data; we're protecting memories, securing intellectual property, and safeguarding the digital assets that matter most to our users.
